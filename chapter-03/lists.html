Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my fav foods. Here is a recipe for deliciouslt rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in pn over med heat
  2. gently mix the eggs and cream in a boel
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Use spatula to fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 secs
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat, it will continue to cook on the plate until served
